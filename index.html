Meet the Proprietress

OLORI ADERONKE ADEOGUN

OLORI ADERONKE ADEOGUN is a visionary leader and a passionate advocate for quality education. She had her first degree in Olabisi Onabanjo University(English Education) She is an eminent peace Ambassador to the United Nations with interest in conflict resolution, widow empowerment, medical aid and support for the less privileged. Her dedication to nurturing young minds and fostering a conducive learning environment has been instrumental in the success of Queen Springs School.

With a strong commitment to excellence, she continues to inspire both staff and students to achieve their full potential and uphold the values of the school.
